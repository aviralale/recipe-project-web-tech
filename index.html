Tasty Momo Recipe

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients for making tasty momo:

  • 2 cups flour (maida)
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 kg minced meat (or vegetable filling)
  • Finely chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • Momo masala
  • Black pepper powder
  • 4 green chilies
  • Timur (Sichuan pepper)

Procedure

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, and water. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling by mixing minced meat (or vegetables) with chopped onions, ginger paste, momo masala, black pepper, and green chilies.
  3. Roll the dough into thin, small circles using a rolling pin.
  4. Place a small amount of filling in the center of each dough circle.
  5. Fold and pleat the edges to create a traditional momo shape, sealing the filling inside.
  6. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes until the dough is cooked and translucent.
  7. Serve hot with spicy tomato chutney or your favorite dipping sauce.

Tips

  • Ensure the dough is rolled thin for the best texture.
  • Don't overfill the momos to prevent them from breaking during steaming.
  • You can use chicken, buff, pork, steak or vegetable filling based on your preference.
